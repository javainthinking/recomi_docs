## Step 1: Setting Up Your Recomi AI Agent

To embed a Recomi AI agent into your [**Hostinger**](https://www.hostinger.com/) website, start by logging into your Recomi account. If you don't have an account yet, you can create one for free. After logging in, configure your agent within the Recomi platform by uploading relevant data sources, such as text file, documents, which will help the agent build its knowledge base.

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth integration of your Recomi AI agent into your Hostinger website.

## Step 2: Generate and Copy Your Recomi AI Agent Embed Code

1. Every agent created in Recomi comes with a unique embed code that allows it to be integrated into your website or application. Once your agent is set up, navigate to the [**Dashboard**](https://recomi.powerdrill.ai/agents) and select the specific agent you want to integrate with Hostinger.

<img src="/images/agent_list.png"/>

2. Clicking on the agent will take you to its agent detailed page. From here, go to the **Integration** section.

3. On this page, you will find the option of **Embed a chat bubble** to Copy Script. Click this button to copy the agent's embed SDK Code.

<img src="/images/agent_bubble_code.png"/>

## Step 3: Embedding the Recomi AI Agent into Your Hostinger Website

1. After copying the embed code, log into your [**Hostinger**](https://www.hostinger.com/) account and access your **Website list**.

2. On your Website list, locate the site you want to embed and click on **Edit website**.

3. Scroll down to any section of the website you wish to add the Recomi AI agent.

4. Click the **Add element** button on the left sidebar of the Website builder. Then locate and click on **Embed Code**.

5. Click the **Enter code** and paste the code snippet into the custom code editor and click **Embed code**.

6. **Preview** or **Update website**, then you should see the floating Recomi AI agent icon on your website.

Congratulations! Your Recomi AI agent is now successfully integrated into your Hostinger website.

## Customizing Your Recomi AI Agent

To personalize the appearance of your agent, go to your Recomi dashboard, select the agent you want to modify, and navigate to the **Playground** and **Settings** tab. You'll find various customization options to adjust the agent to match your branding and service:
- agent response language
- preset questions
- welcome message
- avatar and icon
- colors and visual style
